Just Getting Started?

Welcome to the new and improved Online ChoiceScript Tester!
It's easy to get going, just follow the steps below!

Step #1...
Write (or paste) code to your left.

Step #2...
Click 'Run' at the top!

Step #3...
Use the 'Vars' button to track and ammend variables on the fly!

Step #4...
And click 'Vars' again to return to your code!
